Visualization

This sub-page provide three different visual representations of the findings from our master thesis. The visual components are available with two different data sets; the "old" (TCP) data and the new, unseen data. The first visual component is an interactive bar chart presenting stance distribution per year for climate related publications. The second component provide an interactive relational graph showing the relation between publication stances and common subjects found in the data sets. The last shows the geographic distribution of climate related publications based on author affiliations, sorted by the stance expressed in the abstract.
