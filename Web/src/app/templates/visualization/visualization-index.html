Visualization

This sub-page illustrate three different visual components of the findings in our master thesis. The visual components are divided in old (TCP) data and new, unseen data. The first visual component is an interactive bar chart displaying stances for the TCP climate papers and our predictions toward the new, unseen data. The second component illustrate an interactive relational graph consisting of common subjects of old and new climate papers. The last component display the count of author affiliations in an interactive world map.
